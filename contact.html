Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do I need an appointment?

We recommend scheduling appointments for routine care. Walk-ins are welcome for emergencies and urgent care needs.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Please bring your pet's vaccination records, any medications they're currently taking, and a list of questions or concerns.

Do you accept pet insurance?

Yes, we work with most major pet insurance providers. We can help you submit claims and understand your coverage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and CareCredit. Payment plans are available for larger procedures.
